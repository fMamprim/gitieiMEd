escreve "SMCM" 

Camera control:
- button "W", "A", "S", "D" - camera movement;
- Num5 - Look up;
- Num6 - Change the angle of the right;
- Num4 - Change the angle to the left;
- Num2 - Show down;
- Num1 - Turn right;
- Num3 - Turn left;
- Num0 - Lower chamber;
- Enter - Raise the camera.

"Backspace" - switch the camera off.
